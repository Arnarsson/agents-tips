## News Roundup Guidelines

### Pacing
- **Fast-paced** ‚Äî Short paragraphs, punchy sentences
- **Scannable** ‚Äî Headers, bullets, bold text
- **Opinionated** ‚Äî Your take is the value, not just news aggregation

### Voice
- **Conversational** ‚Äî Write like you're texting a friend
- **Skeptical** ‚Äî Call out hype, question claims
- **Helpful** ‚Äî Signal vs noise (what actually matters?)
- **Fun** ‚Äî This should be enjoyable to read

### Structure Rules
- **Editor's Pick** = 20-30% of the post (the meat)
- **New Launches** = 3-5 items max (quality > quantity)
- **Updates** = 2-4 items (quick hits)
- **Sven's Take** = 15-20% of the post (your voice)
- **One More Thing** = wild card, keep it light

### Time Budget
- Research: 30 min (scan sources)
- Writing: 45 min (first draft)
- Editing: 15 min (polish)
- **Total:** 90 min max
